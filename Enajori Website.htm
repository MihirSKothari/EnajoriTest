We Are Here To ​Tell Stories!

Born out of the passion of storytelling, ‘Enajori’ is ​all about crafting heartfelt tales to tell. Our ​mission is to create narratives that reflect the ​‘human experience’ in all its beauty, complexity, ​and diversity. From thrilling dramas to captivating ​documentaries, every Enajori film is a journey ​into the human spirit. Get ready to feel, think, ​and be inspired.

Niharika Bordoloi

FOUNDER | DIRECTOR

A Director, Writer, Producer who built

her career with advertising, web shows,

documentaries and short films. From

commercial talk shows like What

Women Want to setting grounded

narrative filmmaking, she has made her

mark in the film industry by creating art,

one frame at a time.

Arkish Aftab

DIRECTOR | WRITER

Arkish Aftab is a Writer and Director based

out of Mumbai. Notably, he won 'Best Debut

Director' at the Independent Short Awards

(LA) for his short film Rat in the Kitchen

(2023). With a proficient understanding of

all aspects of film, Aftab honed his craft

under the guidance of re-knowned Ad

filmmaker Suresh Eriyat.

Arun Damodaran

CREATIVE DIRECTOR | DOP

Arun Damodaran is a visual visionary

from Kannur, Kerala. As evident from his

works ranging from Mirzapur, Chandu

Champion and Pendulum, Damodaran

has a pan-india experience working with

the top technicians & filmmakesr in the

country like Sanjay Bhansali, Kabir Khan

and Sudeep Chatterjee.
